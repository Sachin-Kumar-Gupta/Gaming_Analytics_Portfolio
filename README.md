## 📂 Portfolio Projects

### 1. [Player Engagement Analysis](Project1_PlayerEngagement/README.md)
- Analyzed 3K+ players’ engagement metrics including **session count, total playtime, and average session length**.  
- Identified high-engagement players and demographic trends to guide gameplay improvements.  
- Skills: Python, Pandas, Seaborn, EDA, KPI analysis  

### 2. [Monetization & Revenue Analytics](Project2_MonetizationAnalytics/README.md)
- Calculated **Total Revenue, ARPU, ARPPU, and Payer Conversion** for 3K+ players.  
- Analyzed revenue contribution by **Spending Segment, Device, AgeGroup, Gender, and Game Genre**.  
- Skills: Python, Pandas, Revenue KPIs, Cohort Analysis, Data Segmentation  

### 3. [Retention & Churn Analysis](Project3_RetentionChurn/README.md)
- Defined **churn and retention metrics**, analyzed engagement vs churn, and performed **cohort analysis**.  
- Provided actionable recommendations for **early onboarding, VIP retention, and device-specific campaigns**.  
- Skills: Python, Cohort Analysis, Churn Prediction, Retention KPIs, Actionable Insights
